Training

Transform how your organization thinks, collaborates, and performs through the science of human cognitive diversity. Sessions are available both in-person and remotely via Zoom/Skype, or through an internal delivery.

Speaking to Different Thinkers

Effective communication is not universal - it is responsive. This program explores fundamental differences in how individuals process information, organize ideas, and interpret language, giving participants a framework to adapt their communication style to cognitive diversity. Ideal for managers, team leads, educators, and client-facing professionals seeking more resonant, inclusive dialogue.

Cognitive Diversity in Teams

High-performing teams are cognitively diverse. But without a shared language for that diversity, differences become friction. This training introduces applied frameworks for mapping cognitive styles, recognizing complementary strengths, and reducing misalignment. Suitable for leadership teams, cross-functional groups, and organizations scaling culture across distributed or hybrid environments.

Find out more

The Spectrums of Empathy

We do not all empathize in the same way. Some feel deeply, others analyze; some respond with immediacy, others with structure. This session maps the cognitive architecture of empathy and offers practical tools for understanding - and working with - different forms of relational intelligence. Essential for caregivers, HR professionals, therapists, and anyone navigating emotionally complex systems.

Developing 'Interocular' Thinking

Our thoughts are not neutral - they are shaped by cognitive biases, processing preferences, and invisible mental routines. This program builds capacity for metacognitive awareness, helping participants observe their own thinking patterns, question inherited mental models, and cultivate more flexible, context-sensitive reasoning. Ideal for strategists, innovators, researchers, and leaders managing ambiguity or complexity.
